Biography

Sophia (Yufei) Shan is a third-year Ph.D. student in Bioengineering at the University of Washington, advised by Dr. Azadeh Yazdan-Shahmorad. Her research focuses on refining approaches to engineer neuroplasticity for translational neurotechnology applications.

Her work combines electrophysiology, neural signal analysis, computational modeling, and stimulation paradigms to better understand cortical plasticity and guide the design of more targeted neuromodulation strategies. She is particularly interested in neurotechnology, brain-computer interfaces, and translational device development.
